Rules of the game
-Hangman-

Rule 1: the hang is a game which consists in finding a word by guessing what are the letters which make up him. Word which is randomly generated.
Rule 2: the players have each their interfaces but they have the same word.
Rule 3: the players win points of score according to the tries that they their stay.
Rule 4: the winner in a sleeve is the one who confirms the word when he completes the missing letters.
Rule 5: in every bad answers given by the visual keyboard, a try is removed.
Rule 6: the sleeve ends when the word in found summer or when the tries arrive at zero.
