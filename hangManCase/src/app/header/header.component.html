Rules of the game
-Hangman-

Rule 1: the hang is a game which consists in finding a word by guessing what are the letters which make up him. Word which is randomly generated.
Rule 2: the players have each their interfaces but play each their tour to propose a letter on the same word.
Rule 3: the players win points of score according to the tries that they their stay. The players have a timer of 10 seconds to choose a letter, they pass if the limit time is sold.
Rule 4: the winner in a sleeve is the one who confirms the word in the category "to propose a word" or if he completes the missing letters.
Rule 5: in every bad answers given by the visual keyboard, a try is removed.
Rule 6: in every bad answers given in the category "Subject er a word", THREE tries are removed.
Rule 7: the sleeve ends when the word in found summer or when the tries arrive at zero.
Rule 8: the winner is the one who managed to win two sleeves.
